About Workbridge Solutions

Workbridge Solutions connects small and medium enterprises (MSMEs) with trained technical talent from ITIs and vocational institutions. We provide end-to-end recruitment — from candidate sourcing and verification to short-term industry-specific upskilling and post-placement support. Our mission is to make quality technical manpower accessible to local industries while creating sustainable employment pathways for youth.

Our Approach

  • Partner with local ITIs and vocational centres
  • Skill mapping and short-induction training
  • Placement & apprenticeship facilitation

Vision & Values

  • Trust, quality, and local empowerment
  • Transparent candidate verification
  • Partner-first with MSMEs

Services

Manpower Recruitment

Screening, technical shortlisting, and placement of ITI graduates and vocational trainees for MSMEs.

Technical Training

Short induction courses aligned to industry needs — safety, machine handling, basic maintenance.

Apprenticeship & Internships

Facilitation of apprenticeship programs and hands-on internships in local industries.

HR Consulting for MSMEs

Candidate Register & Placement Support

Campus Partnerships
